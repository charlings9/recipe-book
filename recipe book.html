Moi Moi is a traditional Nigerian meal made from beans, onions, peppers, spices, and other ingredients

Moi Moi Recipe
grinded beans
red oil onions crayfish pepper tomatoes egg salt
Making Moi Moi
  • Blend the beans and other ingredients.
  • Portion the batter to the bowls.
  • Put the pot on the stove and add boiled water.
  • Arrange the Moi Moi in the pot and cover.
  • Carefully remove the Moi Moi from the plate and enjoy.

Yam porridge or Asaro is another delicious Nigerian yam dish cooked in a well-seasoned pepper mix until soft and fluffy with some yam chunks

Recipe for Yam porridge
Yam chunks
red oil onions crayfish pepper tomatoes water salt
Cooking Yam porridge
  • Chop the yams into smaller sizes.
  • Put in a clean pot, and add 2 cups (100cl) of water
  • Put the pot on the stove and add other ingredients
  • Allow to heat for 20 minutes or more
  • Your porridge yam is ready, dish out in a plate and enjoy.
